About me.

Here is a bit about me, my drive and motivation.

I am orignal from the caribbean island of Dominca where I lived for 20 years. My passion for technology was sparked at a young age from looking at my uncle repair old radios. I guess at the age of 10 most of my pairs would much rather play sports or video games than obsessing over how a transistor worked. I eventually got into the busniess of repairing electronic applicances in my teens years for neighbors who were crazy enough to let a teenager dissect their appliance. I soon developed an interest in computer technology from this hubby.

Twelve years ago I came to the United States with the same passion that fueled me during my teen years. I guess in life you know that you were born to do something if you have a burning desire that never subsided. My plan of action was to get into tecnology. My IT career consisted of working for Dell as a repair technician, to software and administration and then onsite network tech. I never devoted as much time and passion to my previous IT careers the way I do for Web Development. I have to two daughter who are my motivation for putting in the hard work and effort to not only reach, but go beyond my goals.
